# Limits and Truth

## Fundamental Limit

The camera does not measure internal moisture directly.

It measures optical proxy signals such as:

- reflection
- color
- texture
- clumping
- visible spoilage

---

## What The System Can Claim

- AI-assisted moisture-risk estimation
- AI-assisted grain-quality screening
- crop-specific calibrated visual estimation

---

## What The System Cannot Claim Yet

- lab-grade moisture measurement
- certification-grade moisture result
- universal accuracy across all grains
- validated accuracy without measured labels

---

## Accuracy Claim Rule

Do not claim `80-92%` accuracy unless it is backed by:

- measured moisture labels
- held-out test set
- crop-specific evaluation
- MAE/RMSE
- precision/recall/F1
- calibration error
- false-safe rate

---

## Safe Wording

Use:

"Prototype design for AI-assisted grain moisture-risk and quality estimation. Accuracy pending validation against measured labels."
